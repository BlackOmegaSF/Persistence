
# Persistence

This mod adds the Persistence status, allowing you to keep items after you die.

## Adding Persistence

1. Craft a Reinforced Emerald:\
    ![Crafting box with an emerald in the center surrounded by 9 iron ingots](/misc/Emerald%20Crafting.png)<br><br>
2. Combine your desired item with the Reinforced Emerald in an anvil.\
    ![Anvil dialog box with a stick in the left slot and a Reinforced Emerald on the right](/misc/Emerald%20Anvil.png)\
> [!TIP]
> *The Reinforced Emerald needs to be in the right-hand slot!*
 
Shulker boxes will have Persistence when crafted, and will always be treated as Persistent even if they don't have the status shown.

## On Death
When a player dies, any items that are Persistent will remain in their inventory after they respawn. Persistent items inside shulker boxes will stay in their slot in the box. Any non-persistent items inside a shulker box will be dropped as normal.

> [!TIP]
> If keep_inventory is enabled, all items will remain in your inventory as expected.
